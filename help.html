Help

How can we help?

We are a small team building something we care about. If you have a question, a bug report, or just want to say hello — we read everything.

Using the tools
If you are having trouble with the Cost of Waste Calculator or any other tool, check that you are using a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari). If it is still not working, email us with the tool name and what happened.
Reporting a bug
If you find something that is not working correctly, we want to know. Email hello@mandateopex.com with a description of what you expected to happen and what happened instead. Screenshots help.
Suggesting a tool
The most requested tools get built next. Go to the tools page and use the suggestion box, or email us directly. Tell us what the tool would do and what problem it solves.
Waitlist and early access
If you have signed up and have not heard from us, check your spam folder. We send one email when we launch, nothing else. If you want to update your details, email us.
Get in touch
We aim to respond within one working day.
