About

Built for the businesses that need it most.

The tools that genuinely help businesses improve have always existed. The problem is where they end up. Wrapped inside consultancy engagements, enterprise software, or leadership programmes that cost more than most SMEs spend on a year of training.

The people running those businesses are not short of capability. They are short of access.

Mandate OpEx was built to change that. Over 20 years of operational and commercial leadership across manufacturing, aerospace, defence, capital equipment, and technical services went into the foundations. Practitioners and subject matter experts from across those sectors contributed to the development of every tool, bringing knowledge that usually only surfaces inside large organisations or behind significant fees.

The result is diagnostic thinking that has been tested in real businesses, on real problems, by people who had to make it work.

Good operational and commercial knowledge should not be a privilege. It should be available to any business that wants to grow, improve, and move forward.

That is what this is for.
